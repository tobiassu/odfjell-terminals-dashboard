# Odfjell Terminals – Financial & Operational Performance Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard built to analyze Odfjell Terminals' 
financial and operational performance across eight quarters 
(Q1 2024 – Q4 2025).

## Overview

This project was built as part of my research into Odfjell SE 
ahead of applying for the Business Analyst Summer Internship 
at Odfjell Terminals. The goal was to explore publicly available 
quarterly data and turn it into structured visual insights 
relevant to the Terminals division.

## Dashboard Contents

**Group KPIs – Q4 2025**
- Time Charter Earnings (TCE)
- EBIT
- Net Result

**Terminals KPIs – Q4 2025**
- Terminals EBITDA
- Terminals Occupancy Rate

**Visualizations**
- TCE, EBIT, Net Result & Terminals Occupancy Rate trend 
  (Q1 2024 – Q4 2025)
- Regional Terminal EBITDA breakdown (NNOAT, OTUS, OTK)
  with total reported Odfjell Terminals EBITDA overlaid

## Key Observations

**Occupancy Rate**
Occupancy rate declined from 96.6% in Q2 2024 to 95.2% in 
Q3 2024, driven by softer demand in the US and Asian markets. 
The NNOAT terminal maintained stable performance throughout, 
reflecting its strong position as a key European chemical hub. 
Occupancy recovered to 96.0% in Q4 2025.

**Regional EBITDA**
OTUS consistently contributes the largest share of regional 
EBITDA, reflecting the strong commercial position of the US 
terminals. NNOAT and OTK delivered record results in 2025, 
driven by increased capacity and high occupancy levels across 
both terminals.

**TCE Trend**
TCE declined through 2024 from a peak of USD 215m in Q2, 
stabilizing at a lower level throughout 2025, primarily 
reflecting softer spot rates. The spot market ended 2025 on 
a positive trend, with clean tanker strength expected to 
support chemical tanker rates into 2026.

**Terminals vs. Group**
Terminals EBITDA has remained relatively stable despite 
group-level volatility, demonstrating the resilience of the 
storage business model and long-term contract coverage. 
Reported consolidated EBITDA includes corporate costs and 
non-recurring items – adjusted EBITDA of USD 10.6m in Q4 
2025 versus reported USD 7.9m illustrates the strength of 
underlying terminal operations.
