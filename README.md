# Odfjell Terminals – Financial & Operational Performance Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard built to analyze Odfjell Terminals' 
financial and operational performance across eight quarters 
(Q1 2024 – Q4 2025).

## Overview

This project was built as part of my research into Odfjell SE 
ahead of applying for the Business Analyst Summer Internship 
at Odfjell Terminals. The goal was to explore publicly available 
quarterly data and turn it into structured visual insights 
relevant to the Terminals division.

## Dashboard Contents

**Group KPIs – Q4 2025**
- Time Charter Earnings (TCE)
- EBITDA
- Net Result

**Terminals KPIs – Q4 2025**
- Terminals EBITDA
- Terminals Occupancy Rate

**Visualizations**
- TCE & EBIT trend (Q1 2024 – Q4 2025)
- Terminals Occupancy Rate trend
- Regional Terminal EBITDA breakdown (Noord Natie, US, Asia)
  with reported Odfjell Terminals figure overlaid

## Key Observations

- Occupancy rate declined significantly from Q2 2024, driven 
  by softer demand in the US and Asian markets, while the 
  Noord Natie (Antwerp) terminal maintained stable performance
- Regional EBITDA reflects 100% ownership basis; reported 
  Odfjell Terminals EBITDA reflects Odfjell's equity share 
  across jointly owned terminals
- TCE has declined from peak levels in early 2024 but remains 
  at historically strong levels

## Data Source

All data sourced from:
**Odfjell SE Quarterly Reports, Q1 2024 – Q4 2025**  
Available at: https://www.odfjell.com/investors/reports-and-presentations/

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel / Power Query – data collection and structuring
